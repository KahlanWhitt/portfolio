Building world-class relationships in culture and the arts

Vellum Advising Co. is a boutique consultancy dedicated to elevating collaboration across the global art ecosystem. We connect curators, collectors, investors, museums, and galleries with opportunities that align with their cultural vision and strategic goals.

Whether you’re acquiring, exhibiting, selling, or seeking the perfect piece to enhance your next event, Vellum ensures every relationship is built with integrity, insight, and a deep understanding of the art world’s international dynamics.
